Most builders overcomplicate AI adoption. Start with one repeatable workflow:

1. Capture your daily questions in a single note.
2. Batch them once a day.
3. Ask your assistant for structured outputs: options, tradeoffs, and next actions.

This compounds fast because decision quality improves while cognitive load drops.
